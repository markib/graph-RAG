Customers interact with products through purchases. Each purchase connects a User to a Product and may include metadata such as date and store.

Products are grouped into categories. For example, Laptop and Smartphone belong to Electronics, while Headphones belong to Accessories.

Stores like Amazon and BestBuy sell products. A product can be sold by multiple stores.

Users may purchase multiple products over time, forming a network of relationships that can be queried to understand buying behavior.
